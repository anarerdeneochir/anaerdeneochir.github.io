Botanischer Garten

Rebranding

Based on a fictional project, I created a rebranding for the Botanical Garden in Linz.
My goal was a simple but up-to-date design. Inspired by nature's vibrancy, my design captures the garden's essence through a harmonious blend of organic shapes and modern aesthetics. The dynamic color palette mirrors the changing seasons, while carefullly chosen typography adds elegance.

Wild!

Corporate Identity + Animation

Wild! is a german online content network and is aimed towards people between the ages of 14 and 29 who otherwise hardly or not at all use the television productions of the public broadcasters. I developed the name and logo for this fictitious online content network. For this purpose, I defined a color and shape system as well as typography to create a style guide along with a logo animation
